Google Sheets CMS Connector

You can use Google Sheets as your live Content Management System (CMS). Whenever you edit rows in your Google Sheet, the website automatically loads the latest references!

How to Publish your Google Sheet:
  1. Create a sheet with column headers: Icon, Title, Description, URL, Category, Badge, Visible
  2. In Google Sheets, go to File > Share > Publish to web.
  3. Select your sheet tab and change format to Comma-separated values (.csv).
  4. Click Publish and copy the generated link into the field below.
